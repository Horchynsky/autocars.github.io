About Us

The main activity - the sale of new cars HONDA, AUDI, BMW and more...

Buyers can familiarize themselves with all models in the spacious auto showroom, where all available colors and configurations are presented. All manufactured models can be tested directly behind the wheel.

After a test drive, many customers, having appreciated the excellent ergonomics, dynamics and ease of operation, begin to treat the domestic car in a new way. And, of course, here a wide range of credit and insurance services is offered to customers, without which any modern car dealership cannot be imagined.

For the convenience of customers purchasing a new car, next to the car dealership there is a tuning center in which you can immediately install various additional equipment: security alarm systems, mechanical anti-theft devices, audio system, etc. Moreover, when installing any, even the most "budget" option, experts apply a creative approach, which significantly increases the degree of protection of the vehicle against theft. To give individuality, the appearance of the car can be modified with the help of various tuning elements (moldings, spoilers, protective pads, etc.), according to the wishes of the buyer.
